About Me

Yu Zhuohang, male, Han nationality, was born on July 29, 2004 in Suizhou, Hubei Province. He is a member of the Communist Youth League. He graduated from the School of Science (School of Chip Industry) of Hubei University of Technology in 2026, majoring in integrated circuit design and integrated systems.

During my undergraduate study, I had excellent academic performance, won the first-class scholarship of the university for two consecutive years, and was awarded the honorary title of "college student model", won the national inspirational scholarship, participated in discipline competitions for three consecutive years, and won 10 provincial or above awards, actively participated in extracurricular activities, participated in academic lectures, and did public welfare volunteer activities, with a cumulative volunteer time of 106 hours. In my sophomore year, I founded Hubei Xunhang Technology Co., LTD., and served as the CEO and legal representative of the company.

In terms of student work, I have served as peer tutor of School of Science, Hubei University of Technology, president of IC Association, and assistant tutor of freshman class 24, etc. In discipline competitions and scientific research projects, I have also served as team leader and core member for many times, accumulating a lot of experience in organization, management and teamwork.
